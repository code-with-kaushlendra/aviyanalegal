About Aviyana Legal

Asia's Most Trusted Legal Services Partner

Our Journey

We started our journey as proprietary firm RPG Ventures in 2013 and became Pvt. Ltd. in 2015. From then onwards there has been no looking backwards, the company has embarked upon the success journey in various verticals.

We bridge the gap in working framework, network, regulatory compliance for human resources services, vendors, products & services with affordable and timely legal solutions.

Aviyana Legal was established to meet the growing need for comprehensive corporate legal services at every stage of a company’s journey—from incorporation to expansion. Recognizing the gaps in legal frameworks, compliance, and regulatory support across HR services (factory, office, field), vendor management, and product/service delivery, we offer affordable and timely legal solutions that help businesses operate smoothly and lawfully.

The services from various lawyers or law firms at times are unaffordable and nor available timely as required. Thus many a times the immediate action steps towards solutions not guided is a loss of time and money for corporates.
