Our Service Spectrum: 360° Legal Support

We have meticulously organized our services to cover every facet of your legal needs.

1. Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Our forte is robust representation across all courts and tribunals. We fight tirelessly for your rights.

  • Civil Litigation: Property disputes, recovery suits, injunction suits, specific performance, and contract enforcement.
  • Criminal Defense & Prosecution: Bail matters, anticipatory bail, trials for offenses ranging from theft to white-collar crimes, and quashing petitions.
  • Family Law Matters: Divorce, child custody, alimony, adoption, and domestic violence cases.
  • Consumer Protection: Claims for deficiency in services and unfair trade practices.
  • Constitutional Writs: Filing petitions for the enforcement of fundamental rights.

2. Corporate & Commercial Law

We empower your business with legally sound frameworks and vigorous defense.

  • Company Incorporation & Compliance: LLP, Private Limited, and Public Limited company formation.
  • Contracts & Agreements: Drafting and vetting of Shareholders’ Agreements, Joint Ventures, MOUs, and Service Level Agreements (SLAs).
  • Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A): Due diligence, negotiation, and seamless transaction support.
  • Corporate Governance: Advisory on board responsibilities, CSR, and statutory compliances.
  • Arbitration & Mediation: Out-of-court dispute resolution for faster, confidential settlements.

3. Advisory & Consultancy

Prevention is better than cure. Our advisory wing helps you navigate the law proactively.

  • Legal Audits: Comprehensive reviews to identify and mitigate potential legal risks.
  • Opinions & Legal Notices: Drafting expert legal opinions and effective demand notices.
  • Day-to-Day Business Advisory: Ongoing counsel on employment law, intellectual property, and regulatory issues.

4. Specialized Legal Verticals

  • Real Estate & Property Law: Title verification, drafting of sale deeds, lease agreements, and RERA compliance.
  • Intellectual Property (IPR): Trademark and copyright registration, protection, and infringement lawsuits.
  • Debt Recovery & Insolvency: Actions under the SARFAESI Act, and representation in the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT).
  • Labour & Employment: Drafting employment contracts, handling disputes, and compliance with labour laws.
  • Taxation Law: Direct and indirect tax advisory, and representation before appellate authorities.
  • White-Collar Crime & Fraud Investigation: Defense in cases of economic offenses, cyber fraud, and financial embezzlement.
