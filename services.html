Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Robust representation across all courts and tribunals — we fight tirelessly for your rights.

  • Civil Litigation: Property disputes, recovery suits, injunction suits, specific performance, and contract enforcement.
  • Criminal Defense & Prosecution: Bail, anticipatory bail, white-collar crimes, and quashing petitions.
  • Family Law Matters: Divorce, custody, alimony, adoption, and domestic violence cases.
  • Consumer Protection: Deficiency in services and unfair trade practices.
  • Constitutional Writs: Petitions for the enforcement of fundamental rights.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Empowering your business with sound legal frameworks and strategic protection.

  • Company Incorporation & Compliance: LLP, Private, and Public Limited setups.
  • Contracts & Agreements: Drafting Shareholders’ Agreements, JV, MOUs, and SLAs.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Due diligence, negotiation, and transaction support.
  • Corporate Governance: Board responsibilities, CSR, and statutory compliances.
  • Arbitration & Mediation: Confidential, out-of-court resolutions.

Advisory & Consultancy

Proactive legal guidance to prevent disputes and ensure smooth business operations.

  • Legal Audits: Identifying and mitigating potential risks.
  • Opinions & Legal Notices: Expert legal opinions and demand notices.
  • Business Advisory: Employment law, IP, and regulatory compliance guidance.

Specialized Legal Verticals

Expertise across diverse, complex legal domains.

  • Intellectual Property (IPR): Trademark, copyright, and infringement litigation.
