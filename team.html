Dr Ravindra Pratap Gupta

Chief Executive Officer

Adv. Dr. Ravindra Pratap Gupta is the Founder of Aviyana Legal and also serves as the Chairman & Managing Director of the Aviyana RPG Group of Companies. A multifaceted professional, he is an advocate, author, educationalist, career coach, business mentor, and start-up specialist with years of experience across law, business, and education. He is widely recognized for his ability to merge legal expertise with entrepreneurial vision, guiding individuals, professionals, and institutions toward sustainable success. At Aviyana Legal, Dr. Gupta leads the firm’s mission of delivering professional, ethical, and client-centric legal services while nurturing a culture of mentorship for young advocates and law students. Beyond his legal practice, he spearheads diverse business operations across HR, media, healthcare, infrastructure, education, and incubation, while actively contributing as a thought leader through writing, lecturing, and public speaking. His dedication to career coaching, start-up advisory, and strategic business mentoring has made him a trusted advisor and changemaker in both the legal fraternity and the corporate world.
